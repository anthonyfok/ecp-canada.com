Performance at Spruce Grove Horizon Stage Performing Arts Centre - March 13 & 14, 2014
The students of the Spruce Grove Composite High School performed the musical "Fa Mulan" on March 13 & 14, 2014 at Spruce Grove Horizon Stage Performing Arts Centre. The ECP was invited to play overture music in the Lobby and the theatre before the show, so as to set the mood. One of our members specially did the arrangement of the Mulan movie music "Honor to us all" for this occasion. The audience recognized the song and was really excited about it.

表演歌舞劇「花木蘭」序幕音樂和狄士尼動漫電影插曲 2014 年 3 日 13/14 日

Spruce Grove Composite High School 的學生於二零一四年三月十三、十四日，假 Spruce Grove Horizon Stage Performing Arts Centre ， 演出了歌舞劇「花木蘭」，並邀請了本團派出樂手前往表演序幕音樂，包括了多首富有民族特色的樂曲，還有本團特別為是次表演所編配的狄士尼動漫電影插曲 Honor to us all ，由民族樂器奏出，唯肖唯妙，在場聽眾甚為欣賞，反應熱烈。。
