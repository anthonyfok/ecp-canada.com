 October 12, 2013 Performance For Seniors - Happy Thanksgiving
2013 十月 感恩節音樂會

ECPA members performed at Meadowcroft Senior's House on Oct. 12, 2013 to celebrate the Thanksgiving Day with the seniors. We played seven ensembles such as Full of Joy, Fortum Year, Bow Dance, Love Story, Moonlit in Blooming Flower,Horse Racing and Golden Snake Leaping. All the seniors watched the Chinese style concert with the deepest interest. Some of them had a private consultation with our members to learn more about the Chinese instruments and Chinese music.


在2013年的感恩節之際，愛城民樂團前往Meadowcroft Senior's House向那裡的老人獻上了一台獨具中國民族特色的音樂會。 音樂會上演出了七首合奏作品，有《喜洋洋》、《幸福年》、《弓舞》、《愛的詩篇》、《花好月圓》、《賽馬》及《金蛇狂舞》。老人們饒有興致地觀看了全場音樂會， 并于音樂會後與我們的團員進行了單獨交流，同時也對國樂有了更深入地瞭解。
